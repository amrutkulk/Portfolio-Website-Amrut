Research Paper

Graph Neural Network-based Recommendation System for Job Listings

Jan 2024 - Feb 2024

Summary

  • Objective: Developed a recommendation system using Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) to improve the matching of users with relevant job listings.
  • Graph Representation: Nodes in the graph represented both users and job listings. The edges captured relationships and similarities between users and job postings.
  • Model Training: Utilized synthetic data for training the GNN models. Implemented optimization with the Adam optimizer and Mean Squared Error (MSE) loss function.
  • Recommendation Generation: The trained model generated job suggestions based on learned representations, demonstrating the effectiveness of the system in suggesting relevant job listings to users.
  • System Evaluation: The project showcased the potential of GNNs in enhancing recommendation systems for job listings, paving the way for real-world applications and future improvements.
