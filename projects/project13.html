Spotify Streams 2023 Insights Dashboard

Jan 2024 - Feb 2024

Summary

  • Dashboard Overview: The Power BI dashboard provides an interactive visualization of key performance indicators for the most streamed Spotify songs of 2023.
  • Streaming Trends: It highlights metrics such as the average number of streams per year and identifies the song with the highest total streams.
  • Energy Analysis: The dashboard showcases the average energy percentage of each song, offering insights into the energetic quality of the top tracks.
  • Artist Rankings: It ranks the top 10 artists based on their total streams, providing a clear view of leading figures in the music industry for the year.
  • Top Song Comparison: Compares the streams of the top song with the average streams of other tracks to illustrate its prominence.
  • Track Count: Includes a count of unique track names to reflect the diversity of the top streamed songs.
  • Interactive Visuals: Features various dynamic charts and graphs to facilitate detailed exploration and analysis of streaming trends.
