Summary

  • Custom GAN Model: Developed a Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) with a custom architecture to generate CIFAR-10-like images, consisting of a Generator and Discriminator to create and evaluate synthetic images.
  • Dataset and Training: Trained the GAN model using the CIFAR-10 dataset, leveraging Jupyter Notebooks or Google Colab for training, with support for GPU acceleration to enhance performance.
  • Image Generation: The trained model can produce new images resembling CIFAR-10, with the capability to explore various latent space values for generating diverse image variations.
  • Data Augmentation: Used the GAN-generated images for augmenting existing datasets, improving model performance by adding synthetic data.
  • Concept Exploration: Facilitated the exploration of novel concepts and artistic expressions through the generation of unique images based on the CIFAR-10 dataset.
  • Prototyping and Usability: Enabled the creation of mock-ups and testing interfaces with generated images, with the project being accessible through Jupyter Notebooks or Google Colab, and contributions are welcome.
